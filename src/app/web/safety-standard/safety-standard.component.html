SAFETY STANDARDS –

Welcome to Samadhan App.

The safety of our users is our highest priority. This page outlines the safety standards, moderation practices, and user protections we have implemented to ensure a respectful, secure, and reliable online experience.

1. User Safety & Protection Policies
1.1 Identity & Profile Safety

We take several steps to maintain authenticity and reduce impersonation:

  • Users must register using a verified mobile number or email.
  • Suspicious accounts are flagged automatically using behavior-based risk signals.
  • Impersonation, fake profiles, catfishing, or stolen photos are strictly prohibited.
  • Users can report any suspicious activity directly from the app.
2. Prohibited Content & Behavior

To maintain a respectful environment, the following are not allowed:

2.1 Harassment & Hate
  • Bullying, stalking, threats, degrading remarks.
  • Discrimination or hate speech based on religion, caste, gender, race, or nationality.
2.2 Sexual Content
  • Pornographic content, explicit nudity.
  • Requests for sexual activities, explicit chats with minors.
  • Sharing adult content or sexually suggestive images.
2.3 Violence & Self-Harm
  • Graphic violence or threats.
  • Promotion of self-harm or dangerous behavior.
2.4 Illegal Activities
  • Selling or promoting drugs, weapons, alcohol, or black-market services.
  • Fraud, phishing, or financial scams.

Violations can lead to warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent account ban.

3. Safety Tools Available to Users
3.1 Block & Report

All users can:

  • Report profiles, chats, images, or activities.
  • Block other users instantly to prevent further interaction.

    • Reports are reviewed within 24–48 hours.

3.2 Photo & Content Moderation

We use:

  • Automated image detection (nudity, violence, weapons, minors).
  • Human moderation team for suspicious or flagged content.
3.3 Chat Safety

Chats are continuously monitored using:

  • Machine-learning models to detect abusive or harmful messages.
  • Anti-spam filters for repeated or automated content.
  • Detection of sexual language involving minors.
4. Data Privacy & Security
4.1 Secure Communication
  • All communication (messages, data, uploads) is encrypted.
  • Sensitive data (phone numbers, exact location) is never shared without user consent.
4.2 Profile Privacy Controls

Users can:

  • Hide personal details.
  • Limit who can view their profile.
  • Restrict who can message them.
4.3 Data Storage
  • All data is stored securely according to applicable laws.
  • We do not sell or misuse personal data.
5. Underage Safety
  • Users under 18 years are not allowed.
  • Age verification mechanisms detect underage account creation.
  • Attempted evasion is grounds for permanent ban.
6. User Guidelines & Best Practices

Always:

  • ✔Verify a person before meeting.
  • ✔Meet in public places.
  • ✔ Inform a friend/family before meeting.
  • ✔ Report suspicious or abusive users.

Never:

  • ✘Share OTPs or passwords.
  • ✘Send money to anyone.
  • ✘ Share private photos with someone you don’t trust.
  • ✘ Move to external apps (WhatsApp/Instagram) too quickly.
7. Moderation & Enforcement
7.1 Proactive Moderation
  • AI systems automatically detect and remove harmful content.
  • Manual review team verifies reported cases.
7.2 Enforcement Actions

Depending on severity:

  • Warning.
  • Feature restriction.
  • Temporary suspension.
  • Permanent account removal.
  • Reporting to law enforcement (in extreme cases).
8. Contact & Support

If you feel unsafe, notice harmful behavior, or need assistance:

  • Email: support@shauryatechnosoft.com
  • Emergency: Contact your local authorities
